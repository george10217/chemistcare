About Us

Here at Chemist care, we aim to give all our customers the best service we possibly can. Our aim is to help as many people get their medicine delivered and prescribe as possible to help people living in rural areas who are limited to where they can receive service. Chemist care was founded with our goal of helping you make the best decision for your health. We started as a small business in urban Adelaide and have expanded our services to the rural areas of South Australia. Our mission is to make our patients smile as they leave our stores and return again.

Our staff
